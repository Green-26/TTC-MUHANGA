About TTC Muhanga

Leading Excellence in Teacher Education

Welcome to Excellence

Established in 2010, TTC Muhanga stands as a beacon of quality teacher education in Rwanda's Southern Province. Located in the serene Shyogwe sector, our institution combines traditional teaching excellence with modern pedagogical approaches.

Our motto, "Knowledge, Success and Unity," guides every aspect of our operation. We are committed to preparing teachers who will transform Rwanda's educational landscape through dedication, innovation, and service.

With partnerships including JICA and EAR Shyogwe Diocese, we offer international standards of education combined with Rwandan values and cultural excellence.

Our Programs

Four Specialized Pathways to Educational Excellence

🧒

ECLPE

Early Childhood & Lower Primary Education

Train young minds with play-based learning and child-centered pedagogy

  • Child Development Psychology
  • Play-Based Learning Methods
  • Early Literacy & Numeracy
  • Classroom Management
  • Child Health & Safety
3 Years Duration A1 Level
📚

LE

Languages Education

Master multilingual instruction for Kinyarwanda, English, and French

  • Advanced Linguistics
  • Language Teaching Methodology
  • Literature & Culture
  • Translation & Interpretation
  • Language Assessment
3 Years Duration A1 Level
🔬

SME

Science & Mathematics Education

Develop skilled educators in STEM with hands-on laboratory experience

  • Advanced Mathematics & Statistics
  • Physics & Chemistry

  • ICT in Education
  • Laboratory Management
3 Years Duration A1 Level
