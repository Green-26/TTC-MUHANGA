About TTC Muhanga

Leading Excellence in Teacher Education Since 2010

Welcome to Excellence

Established in 2010, TTC Muhanga stands as a beacon of quality teacher education in Rwanda's Southern Province. Located in the serene Shyogwe sector, our institution combines tradition with innovation to produce world-class educators.

Our motto, "Knowledge, Success and Unity," guides every aspect of our operation. We're committed to preparing teachers who will transform Rwanda's educational landscape through competence, dedication, and passion.

With partnerships including JICA and EAR Shyogwe Diocese, we offer international standards of education combined with Rwandan values and cultural excellence.
