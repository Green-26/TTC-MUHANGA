Welcome to TTC Muhanga

Excellence in Teacher Education Since 2005

Shaping the Future of Education in Rwanda

Located in the serene Shyogwe sector of Muhanga district, TTC Muhanga stands as a beacon of quality teacher education in Rwanda's Southern Province. Our institution is dedicated to preparing competent, passionate, and innovative educators who will shape the next generation of Rwandan leaders.

We offer four specialized programs: ECLPE (Early Childhood and Lower Primary Education), LE (Languages Education), SME (Science and Mathematics Education), and SSE (Social Studies and Religious Education). Each program is designed to meet Rwanda's Competence-Based Curriculum requirements.

Student Placement

Official REB Placement Process

Important Notice: REB Placement Only

TTC Muhanga does NOT accept direct applications. All students are placed by the Rwanda Education Board (REB) through the official national placement system.

ECLPE Placement

Students interested in Early Childhood & Lower Primary Education are placed by REB based on their O-Level results and career preferences.

Minimum Requirement: O-Level with interest in child development

LE Placement

Languages Education candidates are selected by REB based on language performance and national placement criteria.

Minimum Requirement: Strong performance in Languages

SME Placement

Science & Mathematics candidates are placed by REB based on strong performance in Sciences and Mathematics.

Minimum Requirement: Good grades in Sciences & Mathematics

SSE Placement

Social Studies & Religious Education candidates are placed by REB based on performance in Humanities and Social Sciences.

Minimum Requirement: Interest in Social Sciences & History

Placed Students - Welcome!

If you have been placed by REB to TTC Muhanga, please contact our administration for enrollment information and reporting dates.
